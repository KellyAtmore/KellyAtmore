Bee Club

Website and branding concept for Bee Club.

The goal for this project was to create an engaging website that would bring awareness to the issue of bee colony collapse, and build a community for giving and taking action.

I felt that by creating a site that came across as playful and fun, it would be easier to spread a serious and important message.

‍

While issues like this can sometimes seem overwhelming, it is important to not lose hope and to focus on the ways we can help.
